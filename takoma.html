The City of Takoma is the third most populous city in the State of Washington.

Until the city of Takoma was colonized by Europeans, it was already inhabited by the Puyallup Tribe ("People at the bend at the bottom (of the river)").

The city was incorporated on November 12, 1875.

The city of Takoma is the third most populous city in the State of Washington with a population of 223,968. Source

The median income of the city of Takoma was $80,784 in 2022 compared to a state average of $91,306.

Takoma is an urban city with the following geographic makeup:

  • City - 62.42 sq mi (161.68 km2)
  • Land - 49.71 sq mi (128.76 km2)
  • Water - 12.71 sq mi (32.92 km2)
Source

Takoma, Washington is situated along the Puget Sound in Pierce County.
