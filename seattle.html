The City of Seattle is the most populous city in the State of Washington.

Until the city of Seattle was colonized by Europeans, it was already inhabited by the Duwamish Tribe and the Suquamish Tribe. The city was incorporated as a town on January 14, 1865. However, Seattle was disincorporated in January 18, 1867, and reincorporated on December 2, 1869.

The city of Seattle is the most populous city in the State of Washington with a population of 759,915. Source

The median income of the city of Seattle was $115,409 in 2022 compared to a state average of $91,306.

Seattle is located on an isthmus between the Puget Sound and Lake Washington in King County.
