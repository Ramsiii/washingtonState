The City of Spokane is the second most populous city in the State of Washington.

Until the city of Spokane was colonized by Europeans, it was already inhabited by the Spokane People, or the "Children of the Sun". The city was incorporated originally as "Spokane Falls" on November 29, 1881 and reincorporated under its current name 10 years later.

The city of Spokane is the second most populous city in the State of Washington with a population of 229,527. Source

The median income of the city of Spokane was $62,287 in 2022 compared to a state average of $91,306.

Spokane is an urban city with the following geographic makeup:

  • City -69.50 sq mi (179.99 km2)
  • Land - 68.76 sq mi (178.09 km2)
  • Water - 0.74 sq mi (1.91 km2)
Source

Spokane is located in eastern Washington along the Spokane River in Spokane County.
