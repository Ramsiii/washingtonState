Beautiful Washington State is the northernmost state of the Pacific Northwest of the USA.

Washington State was the western part of the Washington Territory that was ceded by the British Empire in 1846 as part of the Oregon Treaty.

Washington State was admitted into the Union as the 42nd State.

The capitol of Washington State is Olympia.
